Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Download Master free to use?

Absolutely. Our core service is completely free. We believe that everyone should have the ability to access and save online content for personal use without a paywall. The service is supported by unobtrusive advertisements, which allows us to maintain the infrastructure and continue developing new features.

Is it legal to download videos?

The legality of downloading videos depends on the content itself and the terms of service of the source platform. Generally, it is legal to download content for personal, non-commercial use (e.g., watching offline), or content that is in the public domain or under a Creative Commons license. However, downloading and redistributing copyrighted material without permission from the owner is illegal in most countries. We encourage all users to respect copyright laws and the intellectual property of creators.

Do I need to install any software?

No. Download Master is a fully web-based application. There is no need to install any browser extensions or desktop software. Everything works directly within your web browser on any operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS).

How do I download a video?

It's a simple three-step process:

  1. Copy the URL: Go to the platform (e.g., YouTube, TikTok) and copy the URL of the video you want to download.
  2. Paste the URL: Paste the link into the input field on the Download Master homepage and click "Fetch".
  3. Choose and Download: Our system will analyze the link and display a thumbnail, title, and available download options. Select your desired quality and format (MP4 video or MP3 audio) and click the "Download Now" button.

Why are some high-quality YouTube videos downloaded without sound?

Modern platforms like YouTube often serve high-resolution video (1080p and above) and high-quality audio as separate streams to optimize playback. Download Master automatically detects this and uses a process called "merging" or "muxing" on our servers to combine the best video stream with the best audio stream into a single, complete MP4 file for you. You don't need to do anything extra; our backend handles it seamlessly.
