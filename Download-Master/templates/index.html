Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Download Master free to use?

Absolutely. Our core service is completely free. We believe that everyone should have the ability to access and save online content for personal use without a paywall. The service is supported by unobtrusive advertisements, which allows us to maintain the server infrastructure and continue developing new features.

Is it legal to download videos?

The legality depends on the content and your local laws. Generally, downloading content for personal, offline viewing (time-shifting) is acceptable, especially for public domain or Creative Commons licensed material. However, downloading and redistributing copyrighted material without the owner's permission is illegal. We urge all users to respect the intellectual property of creators and the terms of service of the host platform.

Do I need to install any software?

No. Download Master is a 100% web-based application. There is no need to install any browser extensions or computer software. Everything works directly within your web browser on any device and operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS).

How do I download a video?

It's a simple three-step process:

  1. Copy the URL: Go to the platform (e.g., YouTube, TikTok) and copy the full URL of the video you want to download from your browser's address bar or the app's share menu.
  2. Paste the URL: Paste the link into the input field on the Download Master homepage and click the "Fetch" button.
  3. Choose and Download: Our system will show you all available download options. Select your desired quality (e.g., 1080p FHD) or format (e.g., Audio MP3) and click the "Download Now" button to start the process.
